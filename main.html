3 Idiots

Actors Quotes

Summary:

In their first year of college, students Farhan Qureshi and Raju Rastogi join the prestigious Imperial College of Engineering (ICE) in Delhi and meet Ranchhoddas Shamaldas Chanchad aka Rancho, their roommate, who is passionate about experimenting with different things, and consequently tops the class. Rancho's carefree approach to education results in him being at odds with the college's director, Dr. Viru Sahastrabuddhe (nicknamed "Virus" by the students),who believes in a strict and competitive education system.

Actors:

  • Aamir Khan as Ranchhoddas "Rancho" Shyamaldas Chanchad
  • R. Madhavan as Farhan Qureshi
  • Sharman Joshi as Raju Rastogi
  • Kareena Kapoor as Pia Sahastrabuddhe, a successful doctor and Rancho’s love interest
  • Boman Irani as Dr. Viru "Virus" Sahastrabuddhe
  • Omi Vaidya as Chatur "Silencer" Ramalingam
